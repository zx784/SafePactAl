RESIDENTIAL TENANCY AGREEMENT

This agreement is made between SunCity Properties LLC ("Landlord") and the tenant
whose name appears on the application ("Tenant") for the property located at
Unit 4B, Sunrise Tower, Al Nahda, Dubai, UAE ("Property").

TERM
This lease commences on the 1st of January 2025 and shall continue for a period
of twelve (12) months. At expiry, the lease automatically renews for successive
twelve-month periods unless either party gives written notice of termination no
less than ninety (90) days prior to the expiry date. Failure to provide such
notice shall obligate the Tenant to pay rent for the full renewal period.

RENT AND PAYMENT
The monthly rent is AED 6,500 payable in advance on the first day of each month.
The Landlord reserves the right to increase the rent at renewal by up to 20%
without further notice beyond this agreement. Rent payments received after the 5th
of the month shall attract a late fee of AED 500 per day until paid in full.

SECURITY DEPOSIT
A security deposit of AED 13,000 (two months' rent) is payable upon signing.
The deposit may be applied at the Landlord's sole discretion to any outstanding
rent, damages, cleaning fees, or administrative costs. The Landlord shall return
the remaining deposit, if any, within 90 days of the Tenant vacating the Property.
No interest shall accrue on the deposit.

EARLY TERMINATION
If the Tenant wishes to vacate before the end of the agreed term, the Tenant shall:
(a) provide a minimum of 60 days' written notice;
(b) forfeit the entire security deposit;
(c) pay an early termination fee equal to three months' rent.
The Landlord may terminate this agreement at any time with 14 days' written notice
if the Property is required for personal use or sale.

MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS
The Tenant is responsible for all maintenance and repairs costing less than AED 2,000,
regardless of the cause. All repairs above AED 2,000 must be pre-approved in writing
by the Landlord. The Tenant is prohibited from engaging any contractor not on the
Landlord's approved list. The Landlord's liability for any failure to maintain the
Property in habitable condition is limited to AED 5,000 per incident.

ENTRY AND INSPECTION
The Landlord or its agents may enter the Property at any time, including weekends
and public holidays, for inspection, maintenance, or showing to prospective tenants,
provided that reasonable notice is given. "Reasonable notice" is defined as no less
than two (2) hours by SMS or phone call.

ALTERATIONS
The Tenant may not make any alterations, additions, or improvements to the Property
without the prior written consent of the Landlord. Any approved alterations become
the property of the Landlord upon completion and must be surrendered at the end of
the tenancy without compensation.

SUBLETTING
The Tenant may not sublet the Property or any portion thereof without the prior
written consent of the Landlord. Consent, if granted, may be revoked at any time
at the Landlord's sole discretion. The Tenant remains fully liable for all
obligations under this agreement regardless of any subletting arrangement.

UTILITIES AND SERVICES
The Tenant is responsible for all utility connections, deposits, and ongoing charges
including electricity, water, gas, internet, and waste management. The Landlord
does not guarantee the continuity or quality of utility services to the Property.

GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTES
This agreement is governed by the laws of the Emirate of Dubai. Any dispute arising
from this agreement shall be resolved by the Landlord's designated arbitration service.
The Tenant waives the right to initiate proceedings in any other court or tribunal.
The cost of arbitration shall be borne solely by the Tenant, regardless of the outcome.

ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes
all prior representations, negotiations, and understandings. The Landlord's verbal
or written assurances made prior to signing are not binding unless specifically
incorporated into this agreement in writing.

By signing this agreement, the Tenant confirms they have read, understood, and agreed
to all the above terms and conditions.
